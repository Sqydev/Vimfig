To make lsp work on nixos you need to install lsp in your config.nix, in this case clangd for c/c++:
	environment.systemPackages = with pkgs; [ clang-tools ]

And then you need to install lsp with mason(clangd in this case):
	:MasonInstall clangd

And mason should use lsp installed by itself located in ~/.local/share/nvim/mason/bin/ (in this case clangd).
